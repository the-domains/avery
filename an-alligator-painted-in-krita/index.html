One of my friends recently suggested some free, open-source art software called Krita, and it’s pretty great! (I’d say that it’s the Blender of digital painting/drawing!)

It’s got a bunch of brushes, and even has a brush stabilizer, which Photoshop doesn’t even have. Honestly, it’s probably on the same level as Sai, only free.
